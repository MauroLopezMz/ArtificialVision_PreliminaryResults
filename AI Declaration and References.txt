Declaration of AI Usage: 

An Artificial Intelligence tool (Google Gemini) was employed during the development of this project. It was utilized as a pair-programming assistant to accelerate the generation of the Python boilerplate code for the synthetic signal mathematical functions, to assist in structuring the CNN layer architecture using TensorFlow/Keras. All underlying methodologies, hyperparameter tuning, and architectural decisions were guided and validated by the author.
